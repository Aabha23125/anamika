Our Expertise in Healthcare Solutions

Accuracy, cleanliness, and durable equipment are essential in healthcare settings. Our goal at Anamika Engineering is to develop solutions that improve daily hospital operations. In order to ensure outstanding performance and a long service life, our products are developed utilizing premium-grade materials, sophisticated manufacturing procedures, and stringent quality checks.

We provide the healthcare industry with confidence, trust, and dependability in addition to selling medical equipment.

Our Key Products

Surgical Scrub Station

It is important that operating rooms be kept hygienic. Our surgical scrub stations are made to give medical personnel, including surgeons, a clean and safe place to wash their hands both before and after operation.

ICU Bed

One of the most crucial pieces of equipment in the critical care unit of any hospital is the ICU bed. ICU beds that are comfortable for patients and as accessible as possible for medical staff have been created by Anamika Engineering.

Electric Examination Table

Multipurpose equipment is necessary for examinations and minor medical treatments. Our electric examination tables are made to be useful, comfortable, and flexible for both patients and physicians.

Autoclave High Pressure Steam Sterilizer

In order to avoid infections and maintain safe medical procedures, sterilization is essential. Our high-pressure steam autoclaves are made to sterilize lab equipment, surgical tools, and dressings.

Dialysis Chair

Since dialysis patients frequently have lengthy sessions, comfort is of the highest priority. The ergonomic design of our dialysis chairs guarantees that patients will be comfortable during their treatment.
